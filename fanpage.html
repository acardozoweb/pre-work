Biography

Jeff Rosenstock was born in Long Island, New York and has been touring with various bands since he was a teenager.

His first band The Arrogant Sons of Bitches quickly gained a loyal following, and that fanbase followed him and grew larger with his next band Bomb the Music Industry!

After that band ended, he has continued writing, releasing and touring original music under his own name, with an ever-loyal following and increasing critical acclaim.
